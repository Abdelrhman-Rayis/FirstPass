MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made between Umbrella Corp and PortSwigger Ltd, each a "party".

1. Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" means non-public information disclosed by one party to
the other that is marked confidential or reasonably understood to be confidential.

2. Obligations
Each party shall keep the other party's Confidential Information confidential and
use it solely for the agreed purpose.

3. Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that is publicly available,
already known, or independently developed.

4. Indemnity
The Receiving Party shall indemnify and hold harmless the Disclosing Party
against any and all losses, damages, costs and expenses arising out of any
breach of this Agreement. This liability shall not be limited or capped in any
way.

5. Term
The obligations shall survive for three (3) years from disclosure.

6. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.

Signed by authorised representatives of the parties.
