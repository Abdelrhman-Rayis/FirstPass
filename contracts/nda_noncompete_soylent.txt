MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into between Soylent Industries and PortSwigger Ltd,
each a "party".

1. Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" means non-public information disclosed by either party
that is marked confidential or reasonably understood to be confidential.

2. Obligations
Each party shall keep the other party's Confidential Information confidential and
use it only for the agreed purpose.

3. Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that is publicly available,
already known, or independently developed.

4. Non-Solicitation
During the term of this Agreement and for a period of twelve (12) months
thereafter, each party agrees that it will not solicit for employment any
employee of the other party with whom it has had contact under this Agreement.

5. Term
The obligations shall survive for three (3) years from disclosure.

6. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.

Signed by authorised representatives.
